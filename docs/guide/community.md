# Community

Zeno is defined by its community of security researchers and open-source advocates.

## Contributing

We welcome contributions! Please see our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/zeno-security/zeno) for guidelines.
*   **Core**: Rust/WASM optimization.
*   **Client**: Browser compatibility and accessibility.
*   **Server**: New storage backends/adapters.

## Support

*   **GitHub Issues**: Bug reports and feature requests.
*   **Discussions**: Architecture debates and help.

## Acknowledgment

Zeno was born from the need for a truly independent, physics-based mitigation strategy, free from the surveillance of major tech conglomerates.
