## Empirical Formulas

**Memory:**
$$\text{Memory (MB)} \approx 32 \times 2^{(GB - 18)}$$

**Time:**
$$T_{\text{total}} \approx T_{\text{base}}(GB) + 3.6 \times VDF \text{ (ms)}$$

Where T_base:
- GB 10-15: ~180-250ms
- GB 16: ~340ms
- GB 17: ~515ms
- GB 18: ~920ms (Default)
- GB 19: ~2100ms
- GB 20: ~3900ms
